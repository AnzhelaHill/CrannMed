About Us

Situated in Ireland’s Medical Device Hub

CrannMed is based in the vibrant city of Galway in the west of Ireland. Within our 6,000Sq/ft facility, our products are developed in our state of the art materials lab and manufactured, lyophilized and packaged in our Class 8 cleanroom.

United in a Common Goal

Our team of Material Scientists, Biochemists, Catheter Engineers and Regulatory Experts have created an innovative technology with a clear regulatory approval pathway. As the innovation progresses, they have been ably supported by Pilot Manufacturing, Business Development and Clinical Specialists who are all working hard towards bringing our products to human use.

Multiple Award Winning

The broad range of complimentary skills coupled with start of the art equipment within a single facility has been critical to the rapid progress we have made within a short space of time. This progress has been recognized with a growing number of external awards and grants.
